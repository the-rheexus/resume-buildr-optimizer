## 1. Lane Recommendation

**Recommended lane: STREAMING**

Reasoning: The role is not streaming-specific, but among the three available lanes
(`STREAMING` / `MKTG` / `STRATEGY`), the `STREAMING` lane is the closest match
because it is the bank's data/BI-heavy lane. The JD centers on collecting,
cleaning, validating, modeling, dashboarding, data-quality checks, Python/SQL/R,
BI tools, and stakeholder-ready reporting. Bank Section 5.6.A already leads with
Revry data analytics: QuickSight/Looker Studio dashboards, Python metadata/API
work, viewership projection, data cleaning/auditing, dashboard QA, and anomaly
investigation.

`MKTG` is secondary because the bank has strong marketing analytics and audience
insight evidence, but the KPMG JD is a general data analyst posting rather than a
campaign/channel analytics posting. `STRATEGY` is useful for stakeholder
presentation and recommendation framing, but it is less directly aligned with
the technical analytics and dashboarding requirements.
